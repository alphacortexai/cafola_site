# Right at Home Website Analysis

## Overview
Right at Home is a senior care and in-home assistance service provider. The website is a professional, service-focused landing page with a warm, trustworthy aesthetic.

## Key Sections & Content

### 1. Header/Navigation
- Logo: Right at Home (with house icon)
- Navigation Links: Services, Resources, About Us, Careers, Contact
- Secondary Links: Medical & Community Partners, Franchise Opportunities
- CTA: Find a location, Phone: (877) 697-7537
- Search functionality

### 2. Hero Section
- Large background image: caregiver with senior client
- Headline: "Right at Home - Let's start talking about living.®"
- Tagline: "Find services in your area"
- Search bar: ZIP code/City, State input

### 3. Services Section - "What We Can Do for You"
Four service cards in grid layout:
- **Companion Care**: Help with light housekeeping, grocery shopping, transportation
- **Personal Care**: Physical assistance, hygiene, mobility
- **Nursing Services**: In-home medical care, ostomy care
- **Specialty Care**: Support for Alzheimer's, diabetes, Parkinson's

Each card has a "Learn more" button.

### 4. Call-to-Action Section
Two-column layout:
- Left: "Already know which services you need?" → "Connect with us" button
- Right: "Not sure what types of care you need?" → "Let's find out" button

### 5. Testimonials Section
Carousel/slider with customer testimonials:
- 3+ testimonials with 5-star ratings
- Includes client name, relationship, and location
- Navigation: Previous/Next buttons and dot indicators

### 6. Our Story Section
- Headline: "Our Story"
- Body text explaining the company's mission and values
- Background image: caregiver and senior client outdoors
- "Learn More" button

### 7. Caregivers Section
- Headline: "Our Nationally Recognized Caregivers"
- Features: "Caregivers of the Year" showcase with photos
- Text: "Each year, we honor caregivers who go above and beyond"
- "Learn more" button and careers page link

### 8. Partnerships Section
- Headline: "We are proudly associated with"
- (Logos/partners not fully visible in analysis)

### 9. Resources Section
- Headline: "Latest Resources for Seniors"
- Featured article with image
- Article cards with titles, descriptions, and "Read more" buttons
- "Latest articles" link

### 10. Newsletter Signup
- Form with fields: First name, Last name, Email
- Checkbox for marketing consent
- Privacy Policy link
- "Sign up" button

### 11. Footer
- Company info: Address, phone, fax
- Links: Find Care, Find Jobs, Contact Us, FAQ, Family Room
- Legal: Privacy Policy, Accessibility, Disclaimers, etc.
- Regional links: Canada, UK, Australia, Ireland
- Social media links: Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn
- Copyright notice

## Design Elements

### Color Scheme
- **Primary**: Teal/Dark Teal (#1B7A7A or similar)
- **Secondary**: Orange/Warm accent
- **Text**: Dark blue/navy headings
- **Background**: White/light gray

### Typography
- Serif font for headings (appears to be serif like Georgia or similar)
- Sans-serif for body text
- Large, readable font sizes

### Imagery
- Professional photography of caregivers and seniors
- Warm, caring tone in images
- High-quality, authentic photos

### Layout
- Full-width sections with centered content
- Grid layouts for service cards
- Carousel for testimonials
- Responsive design

## Key Features to Replicate
1. Sticky/fixed navigation header
