# Right at Home - Design System

## Typography

### Font Families
- **Headings**: Merriweather (serif) - warm, professional, trustworthy
- **Body Text**: Open Sans (sans-serif) - clean, readable
- **System Fallback**: -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, Segoe UI, Roboto, Helvetica Neue, Arial

### Font Sizes & Weights
- **H1 (Hero)**: 56px, weight 400 (regular)
- **Body**: 16px, weight 400 (regular)
- **Links**: 16px, weight 700 (bold)

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Teal/Dark Teal**: rgb(0, 126, 138) - Primary accent, links, buttons
- **White**: rgb(255, 255, 255) - Background, text on dark backgrounds
- **Dark Gray**: rgb(33, 37, 41) - Body text, main content
- **Light Gray**: rgb(245, 245, 245) - Section backgrounds

### Secondary Colors
- **Orange**: Warm accent for CTAs and highlights
- **Navy/Dark Blue**: Hero section background overlay

## Component Styling

### Buttons
- **Primary Button (Teal)**: 
  - Background: rgb(0, 126, 138)
  - Text: White
  - Padding: Moderate (12-16px vertical, 24-32px horizontal)
  - Border Radius: 0px (sharp corners)
  - Font: Open Sans, bold

- **Secondary Button (Outline)**:
  - Background: Transparent
  - Border: 2px solid rgb(0, 126, 138)
  - Text: rgb(0, 126, 138)
  - Padding: Moderate

### Links
- **Color**: rgb(0, 126, 138)
- **Font Weight**: 700 (bold)
- **Underline**: Present on hover

## Layout & Spacing

### Section Structure
- **Hero**: Full-width with background image, dark overlay
- **Content Sections**: Max-width container, centered
- **Padding**: Generous vertical padding (48px+) between sections
- **Grid**: 4-column grid for service cards on desktop

### Hero Section
- **Height**: ~500-600px
- **Background**: Professional image of caregiver and senior
- **Overlay**: Dark teal/navy semi-transparent overlay
- **Text Position**: Bottom-left, white text
- **Search Bar**: White background, positioned below headline

## Visual Elements

### Images
- High-quality professional photography
- Warm, caring tone
- Authentic representation of caregivers and seniors
- Natural lighting, inviting atmosphere

### Icons
- Simple, clean style
- Used sparingly in navigation and CTAs
- Orange accent color for emphasis

### Spacing & Borders
- No visible borders on main elements
- Clean, minimalist design
- Ample whitespace for readability
- Sharp corners (0px border-radius) throughout

## Responsive Design

### Breakpoints (Inferred)
- **Mobile**: < 768px - Single column, stacked layout
- **Tablet**: 768px - 1024px - 2-column layout
- **Desktop**: > 1024px - Full 4-column grid, expanded navigation

## Design Philosophy

The Right at Home website embodies a **professional, trustworthy, and caring** aesthetic. The design choices reflect:

1. **Trust**: Serif headings (Merriweather) convey professionalism and reliability
2. **Care**: Warm photography and teal color create a welcoming, safe feeling
3. **Clarity**: Clean typography and generous spacing ensure readability
4. **Accessibility**: High contrast ratios, readable font sizes, semantic structure
5. **Simplicity**: Minimal design elements, focus on content and imagery

The color palette of teal and white creates a calming, professional environment while the warm orange accents add energy and draw attention to important CTAs.
